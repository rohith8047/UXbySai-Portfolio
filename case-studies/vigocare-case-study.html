01 · Problem

Doctors flying blind between appointments.

Traditional healthcare relies on periodic in-person visits — leaving critical gaps between consultations. When a patient's condition deteriorates at home, doctors have no visibility until it's too late. VigoCare was designed to close this gap with a seamless, always-on monitoring experience.

02 · Research

Understanding the doctor-patient gap in remote care.

Through interviews with clinicians, nurses, and patients managing chronic conditions, three core pain points emerged: cognitive overload from fragmented data, alert fatigue from noisy dashboards, and lack of patient-side context when vitals spiked.

  • Doctors receive vitals data from multiple sources (EHR, wearables, manual entries) with no unified view — creating dangerous blind spots.
  • Alert fatigue is real: 60% of clinical alerts are dismissed because they lack context about trend direction or severity.
  • Patients rarely understand what their readings mean — leading to anxiety or under-reporting of worrying symptoms.
  • Care teams need a quick-glance patient status at ward or remote level, not just individual deep-dives.
03 · Design

A calm, scannable interface built for high-stakes decisions.

The design system prioritises hierarchy and calm — not noise. Critical vitals surface immediately in an at-a-glance dashboard, trend graphs show patterns over time, and smart alerts carry context so doctors can act without digging.
